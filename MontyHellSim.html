How Does This Work?

at first it appears as though the probability is 50/50, there's two doors and one is a winning door and the other is a losing door, however, due to the fact that you are more likely to pick an incorrect door the first time, it is more likely for the alternative door to be a winner, this becomes very clear as you add more and more doors
